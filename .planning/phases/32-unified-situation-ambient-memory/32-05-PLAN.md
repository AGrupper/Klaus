<objective>

daily-remaining header) incremented per primary tick-brain call, read by heartbeat to warn
at 80% of the 200K TPD cap and on `tick_fallback` spikes, with the existing Gemini
fallback taking over at the cap.

metered Gemini. The ledger makes the (small) cost visible early and keeps Klaus judging.

increments on the primary path only + routes to fallback at cap; `check_groq_budget()` in
heartbeat with once/day suppression.
