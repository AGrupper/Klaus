<objective>
Add the two missing data-access primitives the Phase 30 health API routes depend on:
a cross-facet date-range query on `BenchmarkStore`, and a typed Postgres range reader
for the `daily_biometrics` sleep/recovery table. Both follow existing never-raise,
return-`[]`-on-error store discipline.

Purpose: `BenchmarkStore` today only exposes `get_facet_history(facet, n)` and
`get_block_benchmarks(block_id)` (RESEARCH.md Pitfall 2) — the training log needs
"all benchmarks in [start, end] across all 5 facets" in one query. Sleep/recovery has
no range reader at all — `recovery_metrics.fetch_biometric_rows` is a "last N days"
read with a partial column set and must not be modified.
Output: `BenchmarkStore.get_range`, new `core/health_reads.py` module with
`fetch_biometric_range`, and their unit tests.
</objective>
