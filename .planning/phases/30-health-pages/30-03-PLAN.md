<objective>
Build the shared, hand-rolled inline-SVG chart toolkit — `LineChart`, `BarChart`,
`ChartTooltip`, `ChartCard`, `ChartEmptyState` — the first charting capability in the
Klaus Hub. Zero new dependencies (D-04 discretion resolved in 30-UI-SPEC), matching the
`ContributionGrid` "pure hand-rolled visuals, no library" precedent. All three health
pages consume these primitives.

charting library treats "never interpolate, never zero-fill a null" as a first-class
primitive without fighting its API. Hand-rolling gives full control over the SVG path
splitting that makes gaps honest.

in later plans) — this plan ships the toolkit standalone with tests.
