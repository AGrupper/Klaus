<objective>
Expand `UserProfileStore`'s `_SCAFFOLD` from the generic v1 stub (`athletic_goals`, `training_constraints`, `recovery_preferences`) to the v4.0 structured-field contract: `dated_goals`, `weekly_split`, `nutrition_targets`, `supplement_schedule`, `fueling_timeline`, `plan_start_date`, with `schema_version` bumped 1 → 2. </objective>
